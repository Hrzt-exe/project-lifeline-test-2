The Challenge

Global Problems:

  • Millions of children die each year from preventable diseases.
  • Mental health issues like depression and suicide continue to rise.
  • Many people still lack access to clean water, vaccines, and hospitals.

Local Problems (Philippines):

  • Rural areas often lack hospitals, doctors, and medicine.
  • Diseases like heart problems, diabetes, and obesity are becoming more common.
  • Mental health issues among students are increasing, but many donâ€™t seek help due to stigma.

STEM Solutions

  • 1. Using AI in Detecting Diseases - Specialized Artificial Intelligence can analyze medical scans to detect diseases to prevent it from spreading further.
  • 2. Robotics - Advanced technology such as robots can help assist in healthcare, which can improve the public's safety in the medical fields.
  • 3. Biotechnology - Improving equipment on biological systems allows us to develop or create products that benefit humanity.
  • 4. Health Devices - Devices like fitness trackers and smartwatches can help monitor heart rate and physical activity, ensuring that we are able to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Take Action

  • 1. Promote Mental Health Awareness - Make sure to openly talk about mental health and encourage others to do the same to help those who need emotional and mental support.
  • 2. Encourage Healthy Lifestyles - A healthy lifestyle can lead to a longer and happier life and inspires others to do the same.
  • 3. Raise Awareness to Health Risks - Raising awareness can help save lives by helping people to be informed about the risks and make safer choices.
  • 4. Promote Clean and Safe Environments - A healthier environment can help improve each other's lives.
